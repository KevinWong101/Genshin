  1. Gladiator Set and Beserker Sets

    2. The Gladiator Set provides bonus damage if you have 2 or 4 pieces of the set. The Beserker Set provides increased crit rate when having 2 or 4 pieces of the set. These sets are found to be most useful when trying to dish out a lot of raw damage at once.

  2. Instructor and Noblesse Oblige Sets

    3. The Instructor Set if you have 2 helps improve upon your elemental damage and with 4 pieces your elemental combo damage which can scale pretty high. The Noblesse Oblige set increases the elemntal burst damage, which the ultimate or unique move your character can do if you have 2 or 4 pieces. These sets are used to support your party memebers, in order to dish out a lot of elemental damage.

  3. Maiden Beloved and Travelling Doctor Sets

    4. The Maiden Beloved Set provides increased healing done to party memebers and the amount of healing recieved my party members. This effect is only active when you have 2 or 4 pieces of the set. The Travelling Doctor set boosts the amount of healing taken in by 20% with the 2 piece set and with the 4 piece set, your ultimate will restore your health by 20%.
